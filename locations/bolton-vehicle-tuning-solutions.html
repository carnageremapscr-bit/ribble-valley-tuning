AdBlue, DPF & EGR Solutions in Bolton

AdBlue System Solutions

We offer diagnostics, reset, and software solutions for AdBlue systems. Whether you have warning lights, faults, or need a tailored AdBlue software route, we provide safe, legal, and practical support for your vehicle.

DPF Cleaning & Software

Our DPF services include diagnostics, forced regeneration, and software solutions for blocked or underperforming diesel particulate filters. We ensure your DPF system is working efficiently and can advise on the best route for your needs.

EGR Diagnostics & Remapping

We diagnose EGR valve issues and offer software solutions to improve reliability and performance. Our EGR remapping options are tailored to your vehicle and driving style, with a focus on long-term reliability.

Remapping for Emissions & Performance

All remapping is carried out with emissions systems in mind. We provide custom calibrations for both performance and compliance, ensuring your vehicle runs smoothly and efficiently.
